Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that you may have many questions, and we strive to address them to the best of our ability.
That's why we've compiled a list of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) for your convenience. Your questions
and feedback are incredibly valuable to us, as they help us enhance our services and better understand
what matters most to you. We believe that by listening to your concerns and suggestions, we can continuously
improve and provide you with the best possible experience. Thank you for taking the time to share your thoughts with us.

Q. What careers are available in digital arts?

A. Graphic designer, animator, illustrator, concept artist, UI/UX designer, and visual effects specialist.

Q. What skills do i need to become a digital artist?

A. Technical skills, creativity, attention to detail, and understanding color theory, composition and storytelling

Q. What service do art gallery offer?

A. Exhibition space, art consulting/browsing, sales, framing and art appraisal

Q. Do art gallery showcase digital art online?

A. Yes, through our websites, social media, and online platforms

Q. Can digital art appreciate value?

A. Yes, digital art can increase in value over time

Q. What digital formats do galleries accept?

A. JPEG, PNG, MP4, and specialized formats like OBJ(3D)

Q. What types of paintings are exhibited in art gallery?

A. Various style, including abstract, landscape, portrait, still life, and contemporary

Q. Art gallery open to the public?

A. Most of art gallery are free and open to the public

Q. How do art gallery verify artwork authenticity?

A. through documentation, certificates and expert analysis

Q. What types of artworks are exhibited in art gallery?

A. Paintings, sculpture, photographs and mixed media
