Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Directly Provided Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and payment details provided when purchasing artwork or contacting us.
  • Automatically Collected Information: Data such as IP address, device type, and browsing activity when you visit our website or social media platforms.
  • User-Generated Content: Photos, comments, or feedback related to our artwork that you share.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To communicate about orders, commissions, or inquiries.
  • To send promotional materials or updates about new artwork, exhibitions, or events (with your consent).
  • To improve our services and understand audience preferences.
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce our terms.

3. How We Share Your Information

We respect your privacy and will not sell, rent, or trade your information. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • To access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • o request that we restrict or stop processing your information.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect analytical data. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website or social media may link to third-party platforms or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, so we encourage you to review their policies.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date."
